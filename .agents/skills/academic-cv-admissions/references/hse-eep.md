# HSE "Экономика и экономическая политика" CV Rules

Source in Life OS: `projects/masters-applications-2026/requirements/eep_cv_mapping.md`.

Portfolio scoring: transcript/diploma 35, motivation letter 18, CV 15, recommendation 10, English proof 10, academic achievements 12.

The CV section explicitly asks for: education, work experience if any, publications, conferences, seminars, summer schools, grants if any, academic and other achievements, foreign languages, computer technologies, and social activities.

For the user, the CV should be economics/econometrics-facing:

- Move education and research before work experience.
- Show selected economics/math/econometrics coursework.
- Make the bachelor thesis a clear research anchor.
- Include CMF and Yandex Studcamp as academic training/additional education.
- Include conference participation/presentations as CV entries; attach certificates separately.
- Keep ML/work experience, but frame it as pricing, incentives, forecasting, experiments, causal/econometric evaluation, and market analysis.
- Add languages and computer technologies explicitly.
